About Me

I'm a Data Analyst with 4+ years of experience embedded in one of the world's leading football data organizations.

My work sits at the intersection of football knowledge, statistical thinking, and technical execution. At Hudl (formerly StatsBomb), I've worked across analytics, data engineering, and product quality — from building a large-scale cross-platform data integration engine to developing automated performance dashboards adopted across multiple business lines.

I'm equally comfortable deep in Python, SQL, and Tableau as I am collaborating with engineering squads on deployment and iteration. I hold a strong foundation in machine learning and NLP, complementing a sharp analytical mindset with the technical depth to build end-to-end solutions.

What drives me is a simple belief: the best football analysis doesn't just describe what happened — it explains why it matters.
